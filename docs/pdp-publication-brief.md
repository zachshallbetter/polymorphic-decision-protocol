# PDP Publication Brief

Assessment and plan for publishing the Polymorphic Decision Protocol under the Distance from Gray / zachshallbetter.com program, with DOI deposit.

## Novelty positioning (checked against current literature, July 2026)

Adjacent work exists on every individual component; no located work combines them. The neighborhood:

- **Mixed-vendor multi-agent systems**: clinical-diagnosis work studies whether vendor diversity in agent panels improves outcomes (MAC-framework variants comparing model configurations across vendors). Establishes vendor diversity as a live research axis — but as an accuracy lever, not a decorrelation-accounting problem with a consensus-discount formula.
- **Sycophancy in multi-agent systems**: recent work ("Too Polite to Disagree") studies sycophancy *propagation between agents* — how agents cave to peers over discussion rounds, with static and online sycophancy scoring. Closest neighbor to the flip classifier. Difference: PDP formalizes *human-pressure* flips on a single controlled prompt, classifies them into a four-way taxonomy with a mechanical evidence-cover check (claimed new evidence ∉ tool log ⇒ reclassify), and routes the result to an agent-trust ledger rather than a group-consensus mechanism.
- **Constrained LLM agents with deterministic gates**: crypto factor-discovery work uses append-only experiment traces, deterministic engines enforcing selection gates, and auditable hypothesis trails; prediction-market decision-support frameworks argue for LLMs as bounded reasoning engines behind policy gates with human approval thresholds. PDP shares the philosophy; differs in the specific human-custody claims (weight freezing as the anti-sycophancy mechanism) and the invalidation-condition formalism (observable, pre-committed, pressure-asymmetric).
- **LLM trading consensus**: TrustTrade-style selective consensus discounts divergent/weakly-grounded signals. Operates on signal quality; PDP's ACS operates on *input provenance* (two-axis decorrelation), which is a different discount and, as far as located, unclaimed.

**The distinct contribution, stated carefully:** an end-to-end practitioner protocol unifying (a) two-axis consensus discounting (vendor × evidence provenance), (b) a pressure-test procedure with cross-vendor flip classification and mechanical evidence-cover detection, (c) human weight custody as a typed/procedural anti-sycophancy control, (d) pre-committed invalidation conditions with a pressure-asymmetry requirement, and (e) a settled real-money case study documenting a pressure-driven flip occurring, being detected, and being correctly discarded against the eventual outcome.

Honest limits to state in the piece: n=1 case; the win is 80% variance by the protocol's own accounting; individual components have academic neighbors; this is a methodology paper, not a results paper. Those admissions are load-bearing for credibility — the protocol's own §5.3 outcome-blind discipline applied to its debut.

## The asset nobody else has

The case study's evidentiary chain: a timestamped Kalshi trade history + a documented sycophantic flip with same-thread retraction, verbatim in a retained transcript + settlement four days after the flip. (The Gemini/ChatGPT transcripts were stripped before export; the cross-vendor consensus pick survives in the record, the pressure responses do not — claims are scoped accordingly.) Published methodology in this space is almost always retrospective or synthetic. The July 10 flip — an AI abandoning a correct thesis under three words of pressure, in writing, four days before the market it flipped to settled No — is the anecdote the whole piece hangs on.

## Risk register

- **Scooping**: the sycophancy-propagation literature is a week old and moving; the practitioner-protocol angle and the real-money case are the durable differentiators, not the flip taxonomy alone. Mitigation: publish the essay soon; don't gate it on the engine.
- **Overclaim**: any "first" language invites correction. Mitigation: "as far as located" framing, cite the neighbors above, lead with the case.
- **Gambling framing**: a $10 Kalshi bet is the hook, not the thesis. Mitigation: the essay's spine is decision-making under pressure with AI panels; the World Cup is the worked example, and the domain-agnostic section (hiring, vendor selection, product bets) carries equal weight.
